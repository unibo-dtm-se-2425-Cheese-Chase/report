# Known Issues and Future Work

This section outlines current limitations in the game and possible improvements for future releases.

---

## Known Issues

- The behaviour of the cats can still be improved. Sometimes, multiple cats overlap on the same position in the maze, which reduces clarity and can affect gameplay.
- The graphics and animations are simple and functional, but they could be improved to look visually appealing.

---
